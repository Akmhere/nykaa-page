Product Description

Lotus Organics Dandruff Control shampoo is lavishly appointed with 100% Certified Organic Tea Tree extracts to alleviate dandruff & scalp flakiness. Its avant-garde formula hydrates the scalp, profusely nourishes the roots and conditions the hair. It’s a pacifying blend of Certified Organic Australian Tea Tree, Peppermint Oil & Eucalyptus Oils, delivering optimal dandruff control benefits.

This shampoo also behaves as a natural conditioner and does away with agents that cause the scalp to stress & flake.

Key Ingredient

Tea-Tree Oil

Tea Tree Oil is an essential oil is distilled from the leaves of the tea tree, which is native to Australia. Tea tree oil is known for its antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory benefits. These antimicrobial properties make Tea Tree Oil a miraculous cleansing agent. The oil may likewise help soothe irritation brought about by scratching and chemically infuriating the scalp.

  • Natural Antifungal
  • Prevents dandruff build-up in scalp
  • Removes dead skin cells and grime from the scalp

Peppermint Oil

Peppermint oil has excellent moisturizing agents, regular use of peppermint oil will help in long term hydration for the dry scalp. It also helps in increasing the number of follicles, follicle depth, and promote overall hair growth & health.

  • Increases follicle depth
  • Promotes stronger and fuller hair
  • Increases circulation of blood to the scalp

Eucalyptus Oil

It is cold-pressed from trees grown on the verdant slopes of the Nilgiri Hills in Southern India. The antifungal and antiseptic properties present in Eucalyptus oil works against the formation of dandruff. It is also known as the ‘Natural Stress Buster’ essential oil. It has the fresh aroma of menthol and woods calms mental fatigue!

  • Relieves itchy scalp
  • Nourishes hair from the roots
  • Improves overall hair health

How To Use

Step 1

Start with wetting your hair. Do not use too hot or too cold water for this purpose. Simply go for the lukewarm water.

Step 2

Pump out a generous amount of Lotus organics+ Dandruff Control shampoo onto your palm and spread all over your scalp and hair. Massage for 3 to 5 minutes gently with your fingertips.

Step 3

Wash off your hair thoroughly with fresh cold water. Make sure that there is no shampoo residue left on your scalp.
